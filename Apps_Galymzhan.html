Sport training applications

In the modern world where everyone has a smartphone, they use mobile applications. For training and sports, there are many applications that help us and also keep us informed.

These applications will help you train without even leaving home. If you have been practicing for a long time and for you such a concept as training is already understandable, then for such people these applications are just right, but if you are a beginner, it is desirable to train with a trainer.

Below is a list of the 10 most popular free apps:

  • 8fit
  • Map My Fitness
  • Onyx
  • OpenFit
  • FitOn: Fitness Workout Plans
  • Forte
  • Jefit
  • The Johnson & Johnson Official 7 Minute Workout App
  • Strava
  • Charity Miles
